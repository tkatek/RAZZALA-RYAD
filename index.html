Riad Azar Riad Azar is a charming and traditional riad located in the heart of Marrakech. This beautifully restored guesthouse offers an authentic Moroccan experience with its intricate tilework, lush courtyard, and cozy rooms. Guests can enjoy the serene atmosphere, delicious local cuisine, and warm hospitality, making it an ideal retreat after exploring the vibrant streets and souks of the city. Whether you're relaxing by the pool or sipping mint tea on the rooftop terrace, Riad Azar provides a perfect blend of comfort and cultural immersion..
